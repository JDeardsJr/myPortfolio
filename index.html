About Me

As a recent graduate of the Web Developer Track offered through the online bootcamp, Bloc, I have always enjoyed learning how to make things. A few years ago, my passion for theatre led me to attempt to create a website for a fledgling theatre company. Through that project, I discovered a whole new passion for website development that has since transformed itself into an exciting career path!

I am currently based in the Los Angeles area, but am open to relocating for the right opportunity. In addition to spending time with my best friends HTML, CSS, Javascript, and jQuery, I also enjoy hiking, acting, and finding the next series to binge watch on Amazon Prime.
